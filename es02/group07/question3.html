research question

Who are the main actors?

  • Description
  • Protocol
  • Data

Description

In this part we went to search for the most quoted actors of each articles in the three different areas of the last phase. The disposition (top-down) corresponds to the ranking of the articles. If the line is empty, it means that no actor came quoted. The most relevant actors remain NASA and SpaceX, for the numbers of quotation as well as their position in the ranking. Note that Elon Musk is the founder of SpaceX.

Protocol

First, we analyzed manually all the 90 articles and extracted a list of all the actors quoted. We then take the text of each article and put it inside Python. Thanks to the corpus of the articles and the list of actors, we counted the presence of the latter. Then, we keep into consideration the first four more relevant actors, the ones that appear the most and in all the areas: NASA, SpaceX, Elon Musk and ESA. We have then visualized it keeping the ranking of the articles.
