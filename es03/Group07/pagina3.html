How it will work

SpaceX has developed during the years different type of engines which are all loaded with methane propellant. The main reason why have been chosen methane is that is possible to assemble it on the Mars surface, which is rich of carbon dioxide and water, two essential elements to create that substance (credit image below SpaceX).

Wherever you need

Thanks to the Interplanetary Transport System, bases where to land are placed all over the globe. Once landed a spacecraft will be repaired until its last life cycle and reused for another launch and travel to Mars.
